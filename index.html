About

Who I am

I am a Senior Software Engineer at Rolls-Royce Plc. with over 11 years of experience in software development, specialising in web-based model-driven engineering (MDE) tools.

My work focuses on building scalable, collaborative, and user-friendly modelling platforms using technologies such as Eclipse GLSP and Sirius Web.

Previously I was a KTP Research Associate jointly between Rolls-Royce and the University of York, and before that a PhD researcher in the EU-funded Lowcomote Training Network.
